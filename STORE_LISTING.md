## Detailed description

**CoinWatch — Cross-Chain Crypto Watchlist**

Track tokens across Ethereum, Solana, Base, BSC, Arbitrum, and 20+ chains in one lightweight popup. Live prices, market cap, 24h change, and liquidity — updated every 45 seconds.

**Why CoinWatch?**

Most crypto tools are bloated dashboards. CoinWatch is a minimal watchlist that loads instantly, tracks any token on any chain, and stays out of your way.

**Features**

- Cross-chain tracking — ETH, SOL, Base, BSC, ARB, Polygon, Avalanche, Optimism, and more
- One-click add from DexScreener, GMGN, Birdeye, GeckoTerminal, DEXTools, and Defined
- Right-click any contract address on any page to add it
- Price alerts — set thresholds on market cap, price, or 24h change with quick 2x/5x/10x buttons
- Filter by chain — quickly isolate tokens by network
- Favorites, sorting, and search to organise your watchlist
- Light and dark themes
- Keyboard shortcuts for power users
- Import/export your watchlist as JSON
- No account required — works instantly

**How it works**

1. Click the CoinWatch icon or press Ctrl+Shift+W (Mac) / Alt+Shift+W (Windows)
2. Paste any contract address, token symbol, or Binance pair
3. Start tracking — prices update automatically

Or visit any supported chart site and click the floating "Add to CoinWatch" button.

Tip: Customise the keyboard shortcut at chrome://extensions/shortcuts

**Supported data sources**

- DexScreener — real-time DEX prices across all chains
- DexPaprika — automatic fallback for reliability
- Binance — major pair pricing
- CoinCap — market cap and icons for major assets
- Jupiter — Solana token metadata and holder data
